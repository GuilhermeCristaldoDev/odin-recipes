Spaghetti Carbonara

Home

Description

This classic Italian spaghetti carbonara is creamy, comforting, and full of rich flavor. Made with simple ingredients like eggs, cheese, and crispy pancetta, it’s a dish that tastes luxurious without requiring much effort. The sauce is silky and clings perfectly to each strand of pasta.
It’s a great option for a quick weeknight dinner or a special meal when you want something delicious but easy to prepare. With a few simple steps, you can bring a traditional Roman favorite to your table.

Ingredients

  • 300g spaghetti
  • 150g pancetta or bacon, diced
  • 2 large eggs
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan or Pecorino Romano
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente.
  2. In a pan, cook the pancetta until crispy, then add the garlic and sauté briefly.
  3. In a bowl, whisk the eggs with the grated cheese and black pepper.
  4. Drain the pasta, reserving a bit of the cooking water.
  5. Combine the pasta with the pancetta, remove from heat, and mix in the egg mixture.
  6. Add a splash of pasta water if needed to create a creamy sauce.
  7. Serve immediately with extra cheese and pepper.

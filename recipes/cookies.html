Chocolate Chip Cookies

Home

Description

These classic chocolate chip cookies are soft on the inside, slightly crispy on the edges, and packed with warm, melted chocolate. They’re easy to prepare and perfect for sharing—or enjoying all by yourself. The rich buttery flavor paired with chocolate makes them a timeless favorite.
Great for parties, snacks, or a cozy afternoon treat, these cookies bake in just minutes and fill your home with a comforting aroma. They’re a reliable, delicious choice any time you’re craving something sweet.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup softened butter
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 ½ cups chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Cream the butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar together until smooth.
  3. Add the eggs and vanilla extract, mixing well.
  4. Stir in the flour, baking soda, and salt until combined.
  5. Fold in the chocolate chips.
  6. Scoop spoonfuls of dough onto a baking tray.
  7. Bake for 10–12 minutes or until the edges are golden.
  8. Allow to cool slightly before serving.

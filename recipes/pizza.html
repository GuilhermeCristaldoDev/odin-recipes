Pizza

Home

This delicious homemade pizza features a crispy golden crust, rich tomato sauce, and perfectly melted cheese. The combination of fresh ingredients creates a comforting aroma and a flavor that feels both classic and irresistible. Whether you're preparing it for a family gathering or a casual dinner, this pizza brings everyone to the table.
The recipe is simple to follow and customizable with your favorite toppings, from pepperoni to fresh basil or vegetables. With just a few steps, you can achieve a restaurant-quality result right at home.

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1 packet active dry yeast
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ cup tomato sauce
  • 1 ½ cups shredded mozzarella
  • Optional toppings: pepperoni, basil, mushrooms, olives, etc.

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 250°C (480°F).
  2. In a bowl, mix warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it rest for 10 minutes until foamy.
  3. Add flour, salt, and olive oil, then knead until the dough becomes smooth.
  4. Cover and let the dough rise for about 1 hour.
  5. Roll the dough into a circle and spread tomato sauce over the surface.
  6. Add mozzarella and your desired toppings.
  7. Bake for 12–15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted.
  8. Remove from the oven, let it cool slightly, slice, and serve!
